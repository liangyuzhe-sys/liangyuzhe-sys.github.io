Jan. 2026 - Present

Research project advised by Prof. Catherine Chunling Liu at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

  • Using HMC and MALA samplers to generate offline prior-posterior sample pairs.
  • Fine-tuning a pre-trained generative backbone into a one-step analysis-field generator.

Selected Work

Financial Time Series Volatility Breakpoint Detection

Course Project

Built a Bayesian Metropolis-Hastings framework for detecting volatility change points in financial time series under external shocks.

  • Cleaned and preprocessed the 2008 S&P 500 dataset.
  • Implemented likelihood, proposal, acceptance, and multi-chain sampling in R.
  • Produced posterior, trace, change-point, and ESS diagnostic figures.
  • Found the regime shift one trading day before the Lehman Brothers bankruptcy.
